Welcome to SoundGarden Stream Stats!

This dashboard tracks streaming activity for Twitch streamers in the Suno AI music community.

Getting Started:

  • Link your Twitch account to your Discord account in Discord settings
  • Join the SoundGarden Discord Server
  • Start streaming on Twitch to see your stats appear here!

Dashboard Features:

  • Overview Tab: See top streamers and overall statistics
  • Weekly Heatmap: Visualize when streams happen most frequently to optimize your schedule
  • All Streamers: Browse complete list with role badges and detailed stats
  • Time Period Selection: Filter data by 7 days, 30 days, or all time
